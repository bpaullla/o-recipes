Lemon cake

Description

If life gives you lemons, you gotta learn to love them. Unless you're allergic to them. God forbid you consume them if that is the case for you. You just gotta ask life nicely and politely to leave you alone with all the lemon foolery. Of course it will not leave you alone. But you learn to deal with it sooner or later.

Anyways, I love lemonade. I also prefer lemon icing to all sorts of fancy chocolate desserts. So why not love lemon cake as well? You would think this is normal cake with lemon filling but it's actually not. It's got lemon in the cake batter hehe. You'll see.

Ingredients

  • 3 and half cups all-purpose flour
  • half teaspoon salt
  • half teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 and half cups white sugar
  • 1 and half cups unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon lemon extract

You can also add glaze but I never did so I will not copy that part of the recipe, sorry. Also add some lemon zest to the batter. Trust me.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven, grease the pan.
  2. Make the cake: Sift flour, salt, and baking soda together in a bowl.
  3. Beat sugar and butter in a bowl with an electric mixer for 10 minutes. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add flour mixture in three batches, alternating with buttermilk, beating well after each addition and ending with the flour mixture. Stir in lemon extract. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Place in oven, bake, test with toothpick when necessary.
  5. Make a glaze, if you want to. Enjoy the cake.
